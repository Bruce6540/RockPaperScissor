Mastering the Game: Rock Paper Scissors Strategy

Category Insights & Information
Overview RPS is a game of psychology, patterns, and probability. While the computer is random, humans are predictable.
History Originating in China (Han dynasty) as "shoushiling," it evolved into Japan's "Jan-ken." It's now a global decision-making tool.
Winners Stick Statistically, people who win a round are likely to play the same move again in the next round.
Losers Switch People who lose often switch to the next choice in the "Rock-Paper-Scissors" sequence.
The "Rock" Bias Beginners and men have a slight statistical tendency to lead with Rock.
Global Variations Known as Janken (Japan) or Gawi-Bawi-Bo (Korea). Pop culture added "Lizard Spock" to reduce ties.
